# CSS / Frontend Styling Role — HR Interview Questions

## About You
1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Walk me through your portfolio/resume.
3. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
4. What draws you to frontend/UI development specifically?
5. Which project are you most proud of visually/technically, and why?

## Motivation & Fit
6. Why do you want to work here?
7. What do you like about our product's design/UI?
8. Why are you leaving your current role?
9. What's your expected salary?

## Behavioral
10. Tell me about a time you had a tight deadline to implement a complex design. How did you manage it?
11. Describe a disagreement with a designer over feasibility. How did you resolve it?
12. Tell me about a time you caught a visual bug before it reached users.
13. How do you handle repetitive/tedious styling work without losing quality?
14. Describe a time you had to learn a new CSS technique or tool quickly.

## Logistics
15. What is your notice period?
16. Are you open to relocation/remote/hybrid work?
17. Do you have any other offers?

## Closing
18. Do you have any questions for us? (prepare 2–3: design-to-dev handoff process, design system maturity, team structure)

## Tips
- Keep answers concise and structured.
- Tie styling/CSS work to user experience and business outcomes (conversion, accessibility compliance, load time).
- Be consistent with technical/managerial round answers.
- Show genuine familiarity with the company's actual product UI.
